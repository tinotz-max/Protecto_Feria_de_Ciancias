Verbo to-be
The verb "to be" is used to indicate existence, identity, location, and possession. Conjugation:

conjugacion:
- I am - I am
- you are - you are/you are
- he/she/it is - he/she/it is
- we are - we are
- you are - you are/you are
- they are - they are

Examples:
- "I am a student."
- "You are my friend."
- "He is a doctor."
- "We are from the United States."
- "You are going to the park."
- "They are happy."
