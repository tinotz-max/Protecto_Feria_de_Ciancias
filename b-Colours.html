Colors
Information: Colors are an essential part of our world, and they play an important role in our perception and understanding of it. Each color has its own meaning and associations, and they can be used to create different emotions and atmospheres.

Data:
The human eye can perceive millions of different colors.
The most common colors in nature are green, blue, and brown.
Warm colors, like red, orange, and yellow, are often associated with energy, passion, and excitement.
Cool colors, like blue, green, and purple, are often associated with calmness, peace, and tranquility.

Examples:
"The sky is blue."
"The roses are red."
"The grass is green."
"The sunset is a beautiful blend of orange, pink, and purple."
