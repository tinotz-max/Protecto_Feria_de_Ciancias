Information: "There is" is used with singular countable nouns, while "there are" is used with plural countable nouns or singular uncountable nouns.

Data:
"There is" contracts to "there's."
"There are" contracts to "there're."

Examples:
"There is a book on the table."
"There are many cars on the street."
"There is water in the glass."
"There are clouds in the sky."
