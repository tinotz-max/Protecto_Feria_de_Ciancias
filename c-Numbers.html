Information: Numbers are an essential tool for counting, measuring, and comparing. Natural numbers are the positive whole numbers (1, 2, 3, etc.).

Data:
There are 100 natural numbers from 1 to 100.
Even numbers are those that are divisible by 2 without a remainder (2, 4, 6, etc.).
Odd numbers are those that are not divisible by 2 without a remainder (1, 3, 5, etc.).

Examples:
"I have two brothers."
"I can count to ten."
"There are fifty students in my class."
"I bought 3 apples, 2 bananas, and 1 orange."
