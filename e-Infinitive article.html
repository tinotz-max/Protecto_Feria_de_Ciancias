Infinitive Article
The indefinite article "a" or "an" is used before singular countable nouns to introduce them for the first time.

Data:
- "A" is used before nouns that begin with a consonant sound.
- "An" is used before nouns that begin with a vowel sound.

Examples:
- "I have a dog."
- "I see an apple."
- "I want to buy a book."
- "I heard an interesting story."
