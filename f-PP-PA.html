Personal Pronous and Psessesive Adjetives
Information: Personal pronouns are used to replace nouns, while possessive adjectives are used to indicate possession.

Personal Pronousn
-_ I - I
-_ you - you/you
-_ he - he
-_ she - she
-_ it - it
-_ we - we/us
-_ you - you/you
-_ they - they/them

Passesive Adjetives
-_ my - mine
-_ your - yours/your/his/hers/its/ours/yours/theirs
-_ his - his
-_ her - hers
-_ its - its

Examples
- "I like my new car."
- "She has her own room."
- "They are going to their house."
- "This is my book, and that is your book."
